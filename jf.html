Sushi

Sushi (寿司) is a traditional Japanese dish centered around seasoned, vinegared rice (shari), which is typically paired with ingredients (neta) like seafood, vegetables, or meat.

Ramen

Ramen (ラーメ) is a popular Japanese noodle soup dish consisting of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a flavorful, meat- or fish-based broth, often seasoned with soy sauce or miso, and topped with ingredients like sliced pork, dried seaweed, bamboo shoots, and a soft-boiled egg.

Onigiri

Onigiri (おにぎり), also known as omusubi or nigirimeshi, is a classic Japanese comfort food consisting of hand-shaped sticky rice compressed into triangles, rounds, or cylinders, usually wrapped in a crisp sheet of nori (roasted seaweed).
